## Prompt Writing Tips for Album Art

### Basic Prompt Structure:
```
[Style] album cover for [genre] band, featuring [main elements], 
[mood/atmosphere], [color palette], professional, high quality, 
square format, no text
```

### Example Prompts for Rock/Alternative Band:

**Option A - Abstract/Artistic**:
```
Surreal dreamlike album cover, dark moody atmosphere, electric guitar 
dissolving into smoke and light, deep blues and purples with neon 
accents, professional photography style, cinematic lighting, no text, 
square format, 4K
```

**Option B - Graphic Design**:
```
Modern minimalist album cover, geometric shapes, bold contrast, 
dark background with vibrant color splashes, alternative rock aesthetic, 
professional graphic design, clean composition, no text, square format
```

**Option C - Photography Style**:
```
Atmospheric photograph, abandoned industrial space, dramatic lighting, 
moody and introspective, film grain, cool color grading, professional 
band photography, cinematic, no text, square format
```

**Option D - Illustrated**:
```
Hand-drawn illustration style album cover, dark ethereal aesthetic, 
ink and watercolor, abstract figures, emotional depth, alternative rock 
vibe, professional illustration, no text, square format
```

## Step-by-Step: Leonardo.ai Workflow

### 1. Sign Up (Free)
- Go to https://leonardo.ai
- Create account with email or Google
- No credit card required

### 2. Navigate to Image Generation
- Click "Image Generation" from sidebar
- Choose "Alchemy" for best quality (costs more credits but worth it)

### 3. Select Preset
- Choose "Creative" or "General" preset
- Or use "Phoenix" model (newest, best quality)

### 4. Enter Your Prompt
```
Professional album cover for alternative rock band, [describe your vision], 
moody atmospheric lighting, cinematic composition, dark aesthetic, 
high quality photography, no text, square format, 4K resolution
```

### 5. Adjust Settings
- **Image Dimensions**: Set to "Square" (1:1 ratio)
- **Number of Images**: 4 (to get variations)
- **Photo Real**: Turn ON for realistic look
- **Alchemy**: Turn ON for best quality

### 6. Generate
- Click "Generate"
- Wait 30-60 seconds
- Review 4 variations

### 7. Iterate
- Click on your favorite
- Click "Use as Initial Image" or "Prompt Magic"
- Adjust prompt and regenerate
- Repeat until perfect

### 8. Upscale
- Select final image
- Click "Upscale" button
- Choose "2x" or "Creative Upscale"
- Download at full resolution

### 9. Final Check
- Image should be at least 3000x3000 pixels
- High quality, no artifacts
- Looks professional
- Matches your band's vibe

## Adding Text (Band Name/Title)

If you want to add "Altering Gray" or song title to the AI-generated image:

### **Option 1: Canva** (Easiest)
1. Upload AI-generated image to Canva
2. Resize canvas to 3000x3000
3. Add text using Canva fonts
4. Position and style text
5. Download as PNG (high quality)

### **Option 2: Photoshop/Photopea** (More Control)
1. Open image in Photoshop or Photopea.com (free browser Photoshop)
2. Add text layer
3. Choose font (check font licensing!)
4. Apply effects (shadow, glow, etc.)
5. Export as JPG at maximum quality

### **Option 3: Include Text in AI Prompt**
Some AI tools (like Ideogram.ai) can generate text within the image:
```
Album cover with text "Altering Gray" in bold modern font, 
[rest of your prompt]
```

**Note**: AI-generated text can be hit-or-miss. May need manual editing.
