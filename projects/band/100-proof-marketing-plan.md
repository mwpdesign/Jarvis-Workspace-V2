## ðŸš« WHAT TO AVOID (Scams & Ineffective Tactics)

### 1. **Fake Playlist Services**
**Red Flags**:
- Guarantee specific stream numbers
- "Buy X streams for $Y"
- Playlists with suspicious follower patterns
- No transparency on playlists

**Why Bad**: Spotify detects and removes fake streams, can get you banned

### 2. **Bot Followers/Streams**
**Services to Avoid**:
- Anything promising "10,000 followers for $10"
- "Guaranteed viral" promises
- Services on Fiverr selling streams

**Why Bad**: Kills your algorithmic potential, wastes money

### 3. **Pay-for-Playlist Schemes**
**Red Flag**: Curators asking for direct payment to add your song

**Why Bad**: Against Spotify TOS, often scams

### 4. **Radio Promotion for Rock (Usually)**
**Why Avoid**: 
- Traditional radio declining for rock
- Very expensive ($2,000+)
- Better ROI with digital platforms
- Exception: College radio can work

### 5. **Buying "Playlist Placements" on Sketchy Sites**
**Red Flags**:
- $10 for 10 playlist adds
- No curator transparency
- "Instant placements"

**Why Bad**: These are bot playlists or dead playlists
