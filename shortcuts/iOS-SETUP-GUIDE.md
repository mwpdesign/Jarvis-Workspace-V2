## üì± Shortcut 1: "Tell Jarvis" (Voice Tasks) ‚≠ê

**What it does**: Voice dictate ‚Üí Send to Jarvis via Telegram

### **Setup Steps**:

1. Open **Shortcuts** app on iPhone
2. Tap **+** (top right) to create new shortcut
3. Tap **Add Action**
4. Search for **"Dictate Text"** ‚Üí Tap it
5. Language: English
6. Tap **+** below to add another action
7. Search for **"Send Message"** ‚Üí Tap it
8. In the "Send Message" action:
   - **To**: Type `@MWPJarvisBot` (should find your Telegram bot)
   - **Message**: Tap the field ‚Üí Select **"Dictated Text"** from magic variables
9. Tap the **‚ãØ** (three dots) at top right
10. Name it: **"Tell Jarvis"**
11. Under "Siri":
    - Toggle ON **"Show in Share Sheet"**
    - Toggle ON **"Add to Siri"**
    - Record phrase: **"Tell Jarvis"**
12. Tap **Done**

### **Usage**:
- **"Hey Siri, tell Jarvis"**
- Speak naturally: "I need to contact Ryan Koch this week about mastering"
- Jarvis receives it, extracts tasks, categorizes them

7. Search for **"Send Message"** ‚Üí Tap it
8. In "Send Message":
   - **To**: `@MWPJarvisBot`
   - **Message**: Tap field ‚Üí Select **"Provided Input"** from magic variables
9. Tap **‚ãØ** (three dots) at top
10. Name it: **"Ask Jarvis"**

12. Tap **Done**

### **Usage**:
- **"Hey Siri, ask Jarvis"**
- Siri prompts: "What do you need?"
- Say: "What's on my calendar tomorrow?"

## üè† Bonus: Home Screen Widget

**Quick access to Jarvis from home screen**

3. Search for **"Telegram"**
4. Select **"Telegram Widget"** (small or medium)
5. Tap **Add Widget**
6. Long-press the widget ‚Üí **Edit Widget**
7. Select **Chat**: @MWPJarvisBot
8. Tap outside to save

### **Usage**:
- Tap widget on home screen ‚Üí Opens Jarvis chat immediately

## üéØ Pro Tips

### **Lock Screen Access**:
In iOS Settings ‚Üí Face ID & Passcode ‚Üí Allow access when locked:
- Enable **"Siri"** so you can use "Hey Siri, tell Jarvis" even when phone is locked

### **Control Center Shortcut**:
1. Settings ‚Üí Control Center
2. Tap **+** next to **"Shortcuts"**
3. Swipe down from top-right ‚Üí Shortcuts icon
4. Tap it ‚Üí Select "Tell Jarvis" ‚Üí Quick access

### **Back Tap Trigger** (iOS 14+):
1. Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Touch ‚Üí Back Tap
2. Double Tap or Triple Tap
3. Select **"Tell Jarvis"** shortcut
4. Now: Double-tap back of phone ‚Üí Voice dictation to Jarvis
