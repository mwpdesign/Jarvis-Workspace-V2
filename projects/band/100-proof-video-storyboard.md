## üì± PHONE SHOOTING PRO TIPS

### Camera Settings:
- **4K 60fps** for performance (smooth slow-mo options)
- **4K 30fps** for narrative (cinematic)
- **Lock exposure/focus** before each take (tap and hold)
- **Use grid lines** for composition

### Gear Hacks:
- **Stabilization**: DJI Osmo Mobile or cheap phone gimbal ($50)
- **Lighting**: LED panels from Amazon (~$30) or work lights
- **Audio**: Record song separately, sync in post (clap slate)
- **Tripod**: Phone tripod mount ($15) for static shots

### Shooting Tips:
- **Get 3x coverage** of every shot (wide, medium, close-up)
- **Shoot 10-second clips** (easier to manage/edit)
- **Overshoot everything** - you'll have options
- **Use natural light** (golden hour for outdoor scenes)
- **Warehouse**: Use practical lights (work lights, colored LEDs)
