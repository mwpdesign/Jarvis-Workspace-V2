## ‚öñÔ∏è Regulatory Landscape

### **Key Laws:**
1. **FCRA (Fair Credit Reporting Act)** - Governs credit reporting
2. **CROA (Credit Repair Organizations Act)** - Governs credit repair companies
3. **TSR (Telemarketing Sales Rule)** - If cold-calling clients
4. **State Laws** - Vary by state (bonding, registration, etc.)

### **2026 FCRA Updates:**
- Enhanced consumer rights
- Stricter accuracy requirements for bureaus
- Digital identity theft protections
- AI disclosure requirements (emerging)

### **Compliance Requirements:**
- **Written contracts** required
- **3-day cancellation period** mandatory
- **No upfront fees** (must deliver services first)
- **Accurate representations** (no guarantees)
- **State bonding** (varies by state)
