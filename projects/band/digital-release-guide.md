## Phase 2: Distribution Setup (Choose Your Distributor)

You need a **digital music distributor** to get your music on Spotify, Apple Music, etc.

### Top Distributor Options

#### **Option A: DistroKid** (Recommended for Independent Artists)
**Pros**:
- Unlimited releases for annual fee (~$20-$36/year)
- Keep 100% of royalties
- Fast distribution (1-2 weeks)
- Easy to use
- Leave songs up forever
- YouTube Content ID included
- Lyrics sync support

**Cons**:
- Annual subscription (if you stop paying, songs get taken down)
- Extra fees for some features (Shazam, TikTok, etc.)

**Pricing**: $22.99/year (Musician plan) or $35.99/year (Musician Plus)

**Best for**: Regular releases, bands planning multiple songs/albums

---

#### **Option B: CD Baby** (One-Time Payment)
**Pros**:
- One-time fee per release (no annual subscription)
- Songs stay up forever
- Keep 91% of royalties (they take 9%)
- Physical CD distribution available
- Publishing administration available

**Cons**:
- Higher upfront cost ($9.95/single, $29/album)
- They take a small cut of royalties
- Slower distribution (~3-4 weeks)

**Pricing**: 
- Single: $9.95 (one-time)
- Album: $29 (one-time)

**Best for**: One-off releases, prefer one-time payment over subscription

---

#### **Option C: TuneCore**
**Pros**:
- Keep 100% of royalties
- Good analytics dashboard
- YouTube monetization included

**Cons**:
- Annual fee per release ($9.99/year for single, $29.99/year for album)
- Can get expensive with multiple releases

**Pricing**: $9.99/year per single, $29.99/year per album

**Best for**: Established artists with steady income

---

#### **Option D: Amuse** (Free Option)
**Pros**:
- Completely free
- Keep 100% of royalties
- Unlimited releases
- Fast distribution

**Cons**:
- Limited support
- Fewer features than paid services
- "Pro" tier required for some platforms

**Pricing**: Free (with paid Pro option)

**Best for**: Budget-conscious artists, testing the waters

---

### **My Recommendation for Altering Gray**

- Unlimited releases (perfect for singles, EPs, albums)
- Keep 100% royalties

## Phase 7: Marketing & Promotion

### Pre-Save Campaign

**What**: Allow fans to "pre-save" your song before release  
**Benefit**: Song auto-added to their library on release day ‚Üí boosts first-day streams  
**Tools**:
- DistroKid HyperFollow (included)
- Feature.fm
- Toneden
- Show.co

**How to Use**:
- Create pre-save link
- Share on social media
- Add to email signature
- Include in Instagram bio

### Social Media Strategy

**Instagram**:
- Teaser clips (15-30 seconds)
- Behind-the-scenes (studio, mixing)
- Countdown posts (7 days, 3 days, 1 day)
- Release day post with link
- Stories with swipe-up (if 10k+ followers) or link sticker

**TikTok**:
- Use 15-30 second hook from song
- Create challenge or trend
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Release day announcement

**Facebook**:
- Longer-form posts
- Event creation for release day
- Live listening party (Facebook Live or Instagram Live)

**YouTube**:
- Official audio video (static image + audio)
- OR music video (if budget allows)
- Behind-the-scenes vlog

### Email List

If you have an email list:
- Announce release 2 weeks out
- Pre-save link
- Release day reminder
- Thank you + link to stream
