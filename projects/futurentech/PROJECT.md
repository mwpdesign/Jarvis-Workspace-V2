## Analytics & Optimization

### Track Weekly:
- Views per video (what topics/artists work?)
- Click-through rate on artist links (are people discovering?)
- Audience retention (where do people drop off?)
- Comments/engagement (what sparks conversation?)
- Subscriber growth (is the funnel working?)

### Monthly Optimization:
- Thumbnail A/B testing
- Video length testing (5 min vs 8 min vs 10 min)
- Story structure testing
- Topic mix adjustment
- SEO keyword refinement

### Success Metrics:
- **Month 1:** 10 videos uploaded, 500 total views
- **Month 2:** 20 videos total, 2,000 total views, 100 subs
- **Month 3:** 30 videos total, 5,000 total views, 500 subs
- **Month 4:** Hit monetization (1,000 subs, 4,000 watch hours)
- **Month 6:** 10,000+ views/month, $500/month income
- **Month 12:** 50,000+ views/month, $1,500-3,000/month income
