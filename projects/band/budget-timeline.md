## Spotify Editorial Playlist Pitch (Due Feb 17)

**Via Spotify for Artists Dashboard**

### Pitch Template:

**Song Mood/Vibe**:
- [Describe the feeling - energetic, moody, introspective, anthemic, etc.]

**Story Behind the Song**:
- [2-3 sentences about what the song is about, inspiration, or message]

**Genre**:
- Primary: [Alternative Rock / Hard Rock / etc.]
- Secondary: [Post-Grunge / Modern Rock / etc.]

**Similar Artists**:
- [List 3-5 artists that sound similar or inspire you]
- Examples: Breaking Benjamin, Three Days Grace, Shinedown, etc.

**Why It Fits Playlists**:
- "This song fits playlists like [Rock Hard], [New Noise], [Rock Rotation] because [reason - high energy, guitar-driven, anthemic chorus, etc.]"

**Unique Angle**:
- [What makes this song special - killer guitar solo, powerful vocals, unique production, etc.]

## Backup Plans & Contingencies

### If Ryan's Masters Are Delayed
- Have a backup mastering option ready
- Or master yourself using reference tracks
- Build in 3-4 days of buffer time

### If DistroKid Approval Is Slow
- Most approvals happen in 1-3 days
- Contact support if delayed beyond Feb 10
- Worst case: Push release to Feb 24 (still before show)

### If Cover Art Isn't Perfect
- Leonardo.ai is unlimited (FREE)
- Keep generating until you love it
- Canva can help refine and add text

### If Budget Runs Short
- Cover art: FREE (Leonardo.ai)
- Skip paid ads: Focus on organic social media
- Distributor: Use DistroKid basic ($22.99) instead of Plus
