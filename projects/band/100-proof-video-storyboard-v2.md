## üé¨ FINAL CUT PRO EDITING WORKFLOW

### **Organization** (Day 1):
1. **Import all ProRes footage** from SSD
2. **Create Libraries**:
   - Original ProRes Footage
   - Kling AI Enhanced Clips
   - Audio (100 Proof master track)
3. **Keyword Collections** by location:
   - Warehouse Performance
   - Attic Investigation
   - Outdoors Narrative
   - Bottle Smash
   - B-Roll

### **Beat Mapping** (Day 1):
1. Import "100 Proof" audio track
2. Press **Shift + M** on every beat/section change
3. Markers = snap points for magnetic timeline
4. Label markers: "Reel 1 Start", "Chorus", etc.

### **Kling AI Processing** (Days 2-3):
1. **Export 31 priority shots** from Final Cut (H.264 or ProRes)
2. **Upload to Kling AI** (video-to-video)
3. **Process with prompts** (see each shot above)
4. **Download enhanced clips**
5. **Import back into Final Cut** (Kling AI library)

### **Rough Edit** (Days 4-5):
1. **Drag clips to timeline** (snap to beat markers)
2. **Mix original + Kling AI** footage
   - Kling AI for key emotional/visual moments
   - Original ProRes for quick cuts
3. **Build structure** for all 6 reels
4. **Compound clips** for each reel (easy export)

### **Color Grading** (Day 6):
1. **Create LUT from Kling AI** output (match aesthetic)
2. **Apply color wheels**:
   - Reels 1-2: Warm tones
   - Reels 3-4: Cool shadows
   - Reels 5-6: High contrast, muted warm resolution
3. **Add film grain** (Final Cut effect or overlay)
4. **Vignette** on narrative shots

### **Final Polish** (Day 6):
1. **Transitions**: Minimal (hard cuts on beat)
2. **Speed ramps**: Guitar solo moments
3. **Text overlays**: "100 Proof" title treatment
4. **Audio mixing**: Balance music + sync claps

### **Export** (Day 7):
1. **Each Reel** (Compound Clip):
   - File ‚Üí Share ‚Üí Master File
   - Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical)
   - Format: H.264
   - Quality: High (10,000 kbps)
2. **Full Music Video**:
   - Resolution: 1920x1080 (horizontal)
   - Format: H.264 or ProRes 422 (YouTube)
   - Quality: Maximum
